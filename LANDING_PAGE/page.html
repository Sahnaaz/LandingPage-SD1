About SDG 1

The first Sustainable Development Goal aims to end poverty in all its forms everywhere by 2030.

Our Goals

  • Eradicate extreme poverty for all people everywhere.
  • Implement social protection systems and measures for all.
  • Ensure equal rights to economic resources.
  • Address the needs of those in vulnerable situations.
  • Reduce the exposure and vulnerability of the poor to climate-related extreme events and other economic, social, and environmental shocks and disasters.
